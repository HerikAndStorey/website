Herik & Storey Architectural Modelling makes advanced project visualisation models. We are your key to exploring both the inside and outside of a structure, up close and personal, before everything is set in stone.

Our mission, is to provide enhanced design feedback for architects and clients alike.

Architecture is a process of design and revision.
We bring these two steps to life, at a scale that remains effective, and economical.

An image of something we've made - eye catching and impressive.

The models we make incorporate our hallmark modular design, detailed furnishings, and a wide variety of colouring schemes for realism or feature highlighting.

An image of something we've made - showing modularity and colours.

Our process is simple:

  • Create a virtual 3D model.
  • Use advanced 3D printing for the physical model.
  • Render the model's colours by hand.
  • Assemble the model and terrain.
  • Send it to you, fully assembled.

You won’t find models like ours anywhere else.

Each package is suited for a different purpose. Whether you're getting a feel for the design or need a display in the CEO's spacious office, we have the options you need.
